Recipe for soup

This is a recipe for making soup

You will only need a stove and some vegetables

Igredients

  • butternut squash
  • olive oil
  • butter
  • onion
  • garlic
  • vegetable stock
  • creme fraiche

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200 degrees celsius
  2. Cut and roast the squash with a little bit of olive oil
  3. When the roast gets golden and soft, melt the butter and then add the sliced onions and garlic
  4. Cover and cook on a low heat for 15 to 20 minutes until the onions are soft
  5. Add the stock of vegetables and the creme fraiche
  6. If you like the soup more silky, put the soup into a liquidiser if not just wait till it gets like you want and then serve on a bowl
