Recipe to make an homemade hamburguer

alt="Picture of an homemade hamburguer"

This is a recipe for making an homemade hamburguer

You will only need a stoven to make an hamburguer

Igredients

  • Ground meat
  • onion
  • egg
  • burger buns
  • cheese
  • sliced tomato
  1. Tip 500g of beef into a bowl with 1 diced onion and 1 egg and mix
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 burguers in a ball shape
  3. Make 3 cm patties with the burguer balls
  4. Heat the oven and after that cook the patties untik they are cooked each side
  5. After the patties are cooked add some cheese slices to the patties
  6. After the cheese melts on the patties it is time to put it in the burguer buns with some sliced onion, some sliced tomato and then you can add some sauce like ketchup or mayo
