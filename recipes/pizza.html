Recipe to make homemade pizza

This is a recipe for making homemade pizza. You will only need a oven, and if by any chance you have an wood fire oven its even better

You can use whatever ingredients you want but in this one i will show you my favourites.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Feta cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Sliced onion
  • Sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven on 150 degrees celsius for about 10/15 minutes
  2. Prepare the toppings
  3. Put the tomato sauce on the pizza dough
  4. After puting the tomato sauce on the pizza dough it is time to put the toppings in
  5. After the toppings are placed we want to put the pizza in the oven and wait for about 10/15 minutes and then its done
